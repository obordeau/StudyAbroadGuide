What is Study Abroad Guide?

Study Abroad Guide is a mobile application that aims to provide students with a platform to share their experiences and insights on universities around the world. The app is designed to be a one-stop destination for students looking to study abroad, with a wealth of information on each university, meticulously organized on dedicated pages. From accommodation details to course insights, each page is your go-to destination for university exploration.
