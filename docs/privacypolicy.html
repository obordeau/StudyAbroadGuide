Privacy Policy

Study Abroad Guide Privacy is developed by Oceane Bordeau, Darryl Fleurantin, Jay Gomes and Annie Liang for the class CSC 305 at the University of Rhode Island.

Study Abroad Guide accesses the camera's sensor, which is used to take photos, and stores it in the database to complete the user's profile photo.

Study Abroad Guide requires authorization to "access photos and files on your devices" (storage authorization), so that users can upload a profile photo from their device.
